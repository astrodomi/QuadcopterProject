Product page: http://arduino.cc/en/Main/ArduinoBoardProMini

Summary
Microcontroller			ATmega168
Operating Voltage		5V
Input Voltage			5 - 12 V (5V model)
Digital I/O Pins		14 (of which 6 provide PWM output)
Analog Input Pins		8
DC Current per I/O Pin		40 mA
Flash Memory			16 KB (of which 2 KB used by bootloader)
SRAM				1 KB
EEPROM				512 bytes
Clock Speed			16 MHz (5V model)

Software Link: http://arduino.cc/en/Main/Software


USB-TTL to Arduino Wiring diagram
USB-TTL			Arduino
3.3v			DO NOT USE THIS CONNECTION. THIS WILL BREAK THE ARDUINO
5.0v			VCC
TXD			RXI
RXD			TX0
GND			GND

To program the Arduino, hit the "reset" button before clicking upload on the IDE.
